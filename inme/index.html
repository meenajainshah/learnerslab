How travel works

INME pickups are from Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Bangalore and Port Blair. Their team receives kids at the arrival terminal, takes them to the program, and drops them back on return. Book refundable flights.

Unaccompanied minors

Kids under 12 travelling alone must be booked as unaccompanied minors through the airline — or use INME's escorted travel for junior programs (₹2,000 extra, select flights).

Cancellation

Standard: 10% (16+ days out), 25% (11–15 days), 50% (6–10 days), 100% (≤5 days). Premium programs have stricter policies. INME recommends refundable flights.
