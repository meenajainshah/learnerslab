About this camp

The thing that makes this different: you go too. Aavishkaar runs it as a family camp — kids and parents make discoveries together, instead of the usual drop-off-and-pick-up. You're not watching from the sidelines; you're learning alongside.

Five mornings, 8 AM to 1 PM. Breakfast and lunch are part of the day. The activities are hands-on math and science — some indoors, some out in the foothills of the Dhauladhars. Structured but unhurried.

The campus itself is quiet. Trees, hills, no rush. That slowing down is part of the design — it's the point.

A few logistics

Palampur sits in Himachal Pradesh's Kangra valley. The nearest airports are Dharamshala (Gaggal, ~1 hr drive) and Chandigarh (~5 hr drive). Pathankot is the closest railway station.

Accommodation isn't bundled — you'll book your own stay nearby. For stay recommendations, contact Aavishkaar directly.

heads up
This is a parent–child camp, not a drop-off. At least one parent attends every session with the kid. Plan your trip and stay around the full 5 days.
